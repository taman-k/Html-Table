Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Tendulkar, in full Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar, (born April 24, 1973, Bombay [Mumbai], India), Indian professional cricket player,considered by many to be one of the greatest batsmen of all time.
In 2012 he became the first cricketer to score 100 centuries (100 runs in a single innings) in international play.Sachin Tendulkar in India is not just a cricketer, he is God. Also for common people in India, he is an example of hard work and determination.
